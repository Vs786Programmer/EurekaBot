About EurekaBot

Aim of the Project

EurekaBot is designed to help students learn science interactively by providing accurate answers from a structured database of a science book.

Why EurekaBot?

✅ Quick & precise answers from verified content
✅ Interactive learning with AI assistance
✅ No misinformation - Only book-based responses

How Does It Work?

1. Ask a Question - Type a science-related query.
2. Search & Process - The chatbot finds the best match from its database.
3.Get an Answer - EurekaBot instantly displays the correct response

About the Creator

Shubham Khairnar, a passionate web developer, built EurekaBot to demonstrate how AI can enhance education.
